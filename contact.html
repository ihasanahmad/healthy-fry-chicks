Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What's the delivery time?

We deliver within 30 minutes or less to most areas in Haroonabad. Exact time may vary based on your location and order size.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We provide catering for events, parties, and gatherings. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance for catering orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery. Online payment options will be available soon!

Do you have delivery charges?

Delivery is free for orders above Rs. 500. For orders below Rs. 500, a nominal delivery charge of Rs. 50 applies.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! Just mention your preferences when ordering and we'll do our best to accommodate your requests.
